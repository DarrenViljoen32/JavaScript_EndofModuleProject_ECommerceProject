Our Staff

Manager

The Store Manager is a dynamic and knowledgeable professional responsible for overseeing all aspects of the store's operations. With a passion for gaming and an in-depth understanding of the industry, the Manager is in charge of the store's day-to-day operations.

Store Clerk

Our Store clerk is knowledgeable and enthusiastic individual who assists customers in navigating the diverse world of video games. Armed with an in-depth understanding of gaming platforms, genres, and the latest releases, the clerk offers personalized recommendations and guidance to help customers find the perfect game for them.

Administrator

The Administrator is responsible for managing inventory, coordinate with suppliers, and ensure the availability of popular and new game releases. Additionally, they may handle customer service, organize promotional events, and stay updated on industry trends to optimize the store's performance and provide an engaging gaming experience for customers.
