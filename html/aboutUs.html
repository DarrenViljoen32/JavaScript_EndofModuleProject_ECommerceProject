About Us

Find out more about POG Champs!

Our Shop's mission is to be the most customer-centric online shopping destination in Africa, and has built a foundation on the simple concept that the customer comes first.

The success of the business is based on its ability to delight customers with amazing customer service. Our Shop is dedicated to improving the customer experience, from the moment you arrive on the website or app and place an order to when it arrives at your door.

Our Purpose

The purpose of our business is to provide an environment where people of all shapes, sizes can buy Video Games. We strive to provide a place free of judgement and discrimanition. Here at POG Champs there will be no need to hide who you really are. The staff at POG Champs will catter to every customer's needs and wants, no questions asked!

We at the Model Car Shop also participate in a variety of programmes and community projects. Projects such as:

  • Local soup kitchen - where staff help the homeless in feeding them nutrishious meal three times a day.
  • Internet Cafe.
  • A community alternative rock band made up of our staff. Check them out!

Goals & Objectives

The following ate the Goals and Objectives of our bisiness:

  • To provide a judgement free environment.
  • To give people a safe space where they can be themselves.
  • To show fellow video game enthusiests that theres no need to hide themselves.
